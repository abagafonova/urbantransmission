Privacy Policy

Transmission · urbantransmission.org · Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

This website (urbantransmission.org) is a personal academic research platform operated by Anna B. Agafonova, independent researcher. It is not a commercial service and does not collect personal data for commercial purposes.

What data is collected and why

We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts of our own. We do not set cookies, run advertising, or sell data.

However, this site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests a font file, Google receives your IP address and browser information as part of the standard HTTP request. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We use Google Fonts solely for typographic presentation.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). GitHub may collect server access logs including IP addresses as part of standard web hosting infrastructure. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Contact data

If you contact the researcher by email (a.b.agafonova@gmail.com), your email address and message content will be used solely to respond to your enquiry. No email data is shared with third parties.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. As this site does not collect personal data directly, the relevant contacts for data held by Google or GitHub are those services' respective privacy teams. For any questions, contact: a.b.agafonova@gmail.com.

Content licence

Research data, maps, and visualisations on this platform are the original work of Anna B. Agafonova and are provided for academic and educational use. The site's source code is available under the Apache 2.0 licence. For citation and reuse of research content, see the Terms of Use.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the platform grows. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
